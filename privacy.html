Pricing

Simple, upfront pricing.

No hidden costs, no obligation to book. Want an exact figure for your space? Send us a photo and we'll quote it for you — free, no strings attached.

Standard

£250minimum job

Covers our setup, travel and any single image up to 50×50cm (0.25m²).

  • Painted plaster, wallpaper, most smooth interior walls
  • UV-cured ink, lacquer-sealed finish
  • Free design mock-up before you commit

Extra area

£99per additional m²

Once you're past the included 0.25m², every extra square metre is priced at a flat rate — no surprises.

  • Same price whether it's a feature wall or a full room
  • Floors priced the same way
  • Final measurement confirmed on survey

Why is your guarantee 5 years, not 10 or 12?

You'll see some providers advertise guarantees of 10 or even 12 years. UV ink is genuinely durable, but a claim that long rarely holds up once you factor in real-world sunlight exposure, handling and everyday wear. Our 5-year interior / 3-year exterior guarantee, backed by a protective lacquer topcoat, is a number we're confident stands behind every job we do — we'd rather give you a figure you can trust than the biggest number on the page.
