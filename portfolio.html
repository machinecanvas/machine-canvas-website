01 / What we do

Walls, floors,
and everything between.

Machine Canvas prints full-colour, UV-cured images directly onto almost any wall or floor — interior and exterior. Standard flat photographic, or raised textured 3D relief you can feel.

Wall printing

Your walls were always meant to be seen.

Feature walls, retail interiors, reception spaces, murals and exterior facades. Printed in place, edge-to-edge, with zero seams.

Floor printing

The floor is no longer just the floor.

Polished concrete, epoxy resin, tile and wood. Durable, walkable, photographic floor graphics for retail, events and homes.

True photographic resolution

Up to 2,880 × 1,440 DPI — genuine photographic detail at architectural scale, not a blown-up low-res print.

Full-height, single-pass coverage

Prints up to 4.5m tall in a single continuous pass — no visible joins or seams, even on tall commercial spaces and double-height walls.

Efficient turnaround

Around 6–8m² printed per hour, so jobs are completed properly without cutting corners.

Any surface, indoor or out

Concrete, brick, plaster, glass, wood, tile, metal — even textured and uneven surfaces, using the right ink system for each.
